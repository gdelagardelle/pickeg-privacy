Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add player names?

Go to "Add Names" in the main menu, enter a name, and click "Add". You can then select these names when starting a new game.

How do I postpone a game?

During any game, click the "Postpone Game" button. Your progress will be saved and you can resume later using "Continue Last Game".

What are bonus points?

Each player can earn 75 bonus points per round by clicking the "+75" button next to their score input.

How do I win?

The first player to reach 1000 points wins the game!

Where are my scores stored?

All your game data is stored locally on your device. No information is sent to external servers.
